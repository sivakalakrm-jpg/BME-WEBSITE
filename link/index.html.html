Welcome

Welcome to the Biomedical Engineering Department. We focus on merging engineering with medical sciences.

Biomedical Engineering draws on collaboration among engineers, physicians, and scientists to provide new interdisciplinary insights into medical and biological problems.

About Us

The Department of BioMedical Engineering was established in the year 2023 by offering a B.E. program. The B.E. program is conferred with accreditation from NAAC, New Delhi. The department has specialized faculty in the areas of Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, and Microbiology.

Our students are given hands-on training in modern tools of Biomedical Engineering in our laboratories. One of the unique characteristics of the courses offered by our department is that we maintain a limited strength of 60 students per batch for our B.E. courses to facilitate an effective teaching and learning process.

The department provides intellectual effervescence in a highly stimulating academic environment which molds students to readily fit into challenging industrial and academic settings.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  • Motivate students to explore their creativity to develop innovative products.
  • Train students in industrial needs of biomedical engineering through relevant training and value-added courses.
  • Produce technically strong engineers by practicing innovative teaching methodologies.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in Bio-Medical Engineering by imparting in-depth knowledge in Bio-Medical Engineering through Teaching & Research activities.
